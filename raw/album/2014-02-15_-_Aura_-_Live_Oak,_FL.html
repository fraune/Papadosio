about

Papadosio Asheville, North Carolina

Deftly navigating the tricky confluence of prog rock, livetronica, jazz, and jam, Papadosio is a crossover act that has amassed a rabid fan base thanks to a steady stream of albums, hundreds of live recordings, and 17+ years of consistent touring. Papadosio's grassroots operation has grown to overflowing clubs and theaters around the country and cemented their reputation as a must-see act. ... more
